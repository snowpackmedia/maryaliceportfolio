The Miracle Worker - Westlake High School

COVID show. Budgets decimated by school closures. Much was made of what was already available in storage. Dark costumes to start with, then costumes become lighter to symbolize Helen and family's gradual increase of hope. Used my own vision for the crones. Double cast - variation in body type betweeen cast members.
