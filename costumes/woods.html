Into the Woods - Westlake High School

cast of 50 various sized actors for same roles each story had it's own color scheme - baker (orange/yellow), cinderella (silver), jack (brown/green), witch + rupunzel (black/purple), riding hood + wolf (red) "I'm in the wrong story" - clashing colors helps the audience see which characters belonged together
